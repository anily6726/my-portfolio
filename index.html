About Me

Crafting Art
With Purpose.

I'm Anil Yadav, a Senior Graphic Designer and Digital Artist based in Dewas, Madhya Pradesh. With a Bachelor of Fine Art from Khairagarh and over 11 years of professional experience at Aadarsh Ad-Craft, I've built a career on transforming ideas into unforgettable visual experiences.

My specialities span the full creative spectrum — from hyper-realistic digital paintings and expressive caricatures, to clean line art, stencil design, cartoon illustration, and B&W mascot characters. Every piece is crafted with intention and precision.
